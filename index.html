About

Foothill Bee Ranch raises blue orchard bees (Osmia lignaria) sustainably for pollination of crops in California. Our bees are sourced from populations native to the Sierra foothills and will only be sold in California.

Foothill Bee Ranch is owned and operated by Steve Peterson.

Publications:

[1] Peterson, S. (2013). Managing blue orchard bees in pollination. Bee Culture. Nov. Issue, p. 25-29.

[2] Peterson, S. S. & D. R. Artz. (2014). Production of solitary bees for pollination in the United States. In Mass Production of Beneficial Organisms: Invertebrates and Entomopathogens. (eds. J. A. Morales-Ramos, M. G. Rojas & D. I. Shapiro-Ilan), Elsevier Science, London.
