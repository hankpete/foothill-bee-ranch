About

Foothill Bee Ranch raises blue orchard bees (Osmia lignaria) sustainably for pollination of crops in California. Our bees are sourced from populations native to the Sierra foothills and will only be sold in California.

The ranch's founder, Steve Peterson, has a Ph.D. in (is it biology?? idr). He has conducted extensive research on blue orchard bees and his writing has been published in textbooks and scientific journals.
